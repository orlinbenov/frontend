Fourth – Mobile/UI JavaScript Development Exercise 

This exercise should not take longer than 2 hours to complete 


View Take Home Pay, Income Tax & National Insurance



Scenario
The Fourth Mobile/UI development team have been requested to build an application that works on both web and mobile devices to view take home pay after income tax and national insurance for a list of internal staff members. The application is also required to display a breakdown of the amount of income tax and national insurance paid.

User Story
As a customer  I want to view the amount of income tax and national insurance I pay So that I know my take home annual salary after deductions

Acceptance Criteria
Employee data should match the ‘Employees’ table below
Find employee by ‘First Name’ or ‘Surname’ using autocomplete on an input field
Tax and salary calculation data is taken from the ‘Employees’ table below
National insurance calculations are based on the ‘National Insurance Data’ table below
Total annual take home pay after deductions are displayed on a HTML page
Total annual income tax paid should be displayed on a HTML page
Total annual national insurance paid should be displayed on a HTML page

Tech requirements
Built with a MV* JavaScript framework (AngularJS)
Restful API to GET employee data and calculations 
HTML5, CSS3 
Unit Tests (Bonus points for E2E tests)
